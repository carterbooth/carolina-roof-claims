What Does Tree Fell on Roof Mean in Cary?

When a tree falls on your roof, the damage can range from superficial to catastrophic. Tree impact typically causes:

  • Structural damage - Broken rafters, damaged roof decking, or compromised support systems
  • Immediate water exposure - Large openings that allow rain and weather into your home
  • Interior damage - Ceiling collapse, damaged belongings, electrical hazards
  • Surrounding damage - Areas around impact may have hidden stress damage

In {{CITY}}, tree damage is common during high winds, ice storms, and severe weather when large trees become unstable.

Don't Wait - Act Fast

Tree damage to roofs is an emergency situation requiring immediate professional assessment and temporary protection. Exposed openings allow continuing weather damage and pose safety risks.

What You Should Do Right Now:

  1. Document everything - Take photos of the tree damage
  2. Check for other signs - Look for additional damage
  3. Contact a professional - Get a certified inspection
  4. Call your insurance - Report potential storm damage
