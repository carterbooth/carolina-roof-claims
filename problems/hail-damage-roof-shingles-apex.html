What Does Hail Damage on Roof Shingles Mean in Apex?

Hail damage to roof shingles may not be immediately obvious but can severely compromise your roof's integrity. Hail impact causes:

  • Granule loss - Protective granules knocked off, exposing asphalt layer
  • Mat fractures - Breaks in the fiberglass mat that forms the shingle's structure
  • Exposed adhesive - Dark spots where granules are missing
  • Accelerated aging - Damaged shingles deteriorate much faster than normal
